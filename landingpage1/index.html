How is quouch different?

Likeminded people that respect all forms of living use our platform to travel safely, experience new places in a unique way, get in touch with the local community and sleep at friendly peoples homes. Hosts offer a safe place to stay and no room for misunderstandings. No need to stress about setting boundaries or getting into uncomfortable sleep over situations.

With our carefully designed algorithm we match travelers and hosts according to Humor, Personality and Interests.

About

We are an all female founders team that used to travel using the homestay platform Couchsurfing a lot. At some point we got so annoyed by male hosts crossing boundaries that we stopped using it. Not being ABLE TO TRAVEL AS FREE AND SAFE AS A WOMAN COMPARED TO MEN IS A STRUCTURAL PROBLEM. Eventually, we realized that this does not have to be like this. And that we can create a new, much better platform. This is how Quouch came into being.

WE BELIEVE IN SAFE AND FREE TRAVEL FOR FEMALE AND QUEER solo TRAVELLERS. NO stress, no limits, NO anmache.
